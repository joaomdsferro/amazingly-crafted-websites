Honorable Mentions/"Lessons learned":

* [Family.co](https://family.co/) - Never used it, but the animations look extremely good on the performance side and the design is neat!
* [Amie](https://amie.so/) - Not much going on here in terms of design specifically, but VERY GOOD positioning of subtle icons/animations. Makes a page feel better immediately 
* [Off-menu](https://www.offmenu.design/) - Amazing ideas, well crafted. Too much going on for a new user. No sense of purpose for someone who just found out this website.
* [Shader](https://www.shader.se/) - Very nice retro-styled 3d website! Still think that a marketing homepage should be simpler... But it's very cool!
